# Worldwide Window — Premium Window Cleaning Website

## 1. Project Description
A high-converting, visually premium, SEO-optimized, AI-search and voice-search ready website for **Worldwide Window**, a professional window cleaning company serving residential and commercial clients in the Los Angeles Metro Area. The site is designed to out-perform competitors through superior design, conversion optimization, and local SEO authority.

## 2. Page Structure
- `/` — Homepage (hero, trust bar, services, why us, about, testimonials, areas, process, FAQ, gallery, lead form)
- `/about` — About Us
- `/services` — Services Overview
- `/service-areas` — Service Areas
- `/gallery` — Photo Gallery
- `/reviews` — Customer Reviews
- `/faq` — FAQ Page
- `/contact` — Contact & Quote Page
- `/process` — Our Process
